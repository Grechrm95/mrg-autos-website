About MRG AUTOS

How It All Began...

For us, it all started with a shared passion. MRG AUTOS was born from a family of petrol heads whose hobby was tracking down classic cars that needed a little restoration. We loved the thrill of bringing them back to their former glory, selling them on to new owners, and occasionally adding a new gem to our own collection.

This hobby soon grew into a mission: to provide reliable, everyday vehicles at affordable prices to our community. While we focus on a carefully curated selection rather than a vast forecourt, our promise is to offer exceptional value and some of the best prices you'll find.

Our Promise to You

Our roots as hobbyists and enthusiasts define how we do business. We believe in quality over quantity and honest, transparent dealing. Every vehicle we source is one we'd be proud to own ourselves. We're not just selling cars; we're sharing our passion and helping you find a vehicle you can trust and enjoy.

Why Choose MRG AUTOS?

  • Passionate & Knowledgeable Team: As a family of "petrol heads," we know cars inside and out and are dedicated to finding the right one for you.
  • Quality Over Quantity: Every car is hand-picked and carefully inspected to meet our high standards of quality and reliability.
  • Affordable & Transparent Pricing: We believe in fair prices with no hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay.
  • Customer-Focused Service: We started this to serve the everyday person, and your satisfaction is our top priority.
